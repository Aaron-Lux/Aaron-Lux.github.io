About Aaron Lux

I'm dedicated to clear explanations of predictive machine learning programming from a computation undarstanding-first, mathematics-second point of view.

Recurrent Neural Networks offer a natural approach to sequence generation, yet it is hidden from readers behind chapters of slow, mathematical analysis. I believe the mathematical intractability of RNN models is causing developers to only solve the most simple low-value business problems. This can leave businesses with a so-what feeling about recurrent neural networks.

This paper is designed as an introduction to recurrent neural networks (and other sequence generators) from a computational understanding-first, mathematics-second, point of view. For the reader who is simply interested in the practice of recurrent neural networks, this text should be sufficient and entertaining.
